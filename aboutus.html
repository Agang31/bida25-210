About Us

Discover our story

Puppy Love – The Pawfect Home is a community-based, non‑profit initiative dedicated to rescuing, caring for, and rehoming lost, stray, and abandoned puppies within Gaborone and its surrounding areas. Driven by compassion for vulnerable animals, Puppy Love conducts weekly rescue efforts across local communities, ensuring puppies found on the streets are brought to safety and given essential care, including feeding, cleaning, and medical attention.

Although Puppy Love was established in 2020, the website was just recently developed in 2026 to digitilize and support this mission by serving as a reliable and accessible online platform that bridges the gap between rescued puppies and potential adopters. The website showcases puppies available for adoption from the Puppy Love shelter, provides guidance on responsible pet ownership, and raises awareness about animal welfare within the local community.

In addition to adoption services, the platform supports Puppy Love’s rescue efforts by encouraging donations and sponsorships from individuals, businesses, and animal welfare supporters who wish to contribute to the wellbeing of rescued puppies. Through awareness, community involvement, and compassion, Puppy Love – The Pawfect Home strives to reduce the number of stray puppies and create a future where every puppy has a home filled with love and care.
