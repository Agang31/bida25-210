Puppy Love regularly host events such as puppy adoption events, pet talent show and pet parades so as to raise awareness as well as raise funds and encourage community engagement.

How we use our donations

  • Providing food and basic needs for the animals
  • Veterinary care and medical expenses
  • Shelter operations and facility maintenance

By donating to Puppy Love you are directly contributing to the lives of contless puppies and helping them find forever homes.
